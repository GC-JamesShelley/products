This section covers the risks associated with the use of benzodiazepines in special circumstances. The topics included are:

* Pregnancy and breastfeeding
* Elderly
* Benzodiazepines for the management of alcohol withdrawal
This learning module discusses noteworthy risks for benzodiazepines. Summaries of product characteristics and the BNF should be consulted for a fuller account of the adverse effects and warnings for individual benzodiazepines.
